Safety & Accountability

Governance & System Boundaries

System Mandate
"Klara is a navigation system designed to guide access to care. It does not diagnose medical conditions and does not replace clinical judgement. Emergency indicators always override system routing and cannot be suppressed by any operator or configuration."
🔒

No Diagnostic Authority

Klara classifies urgency signals, not medical conditions. All diagnostic authority remains with licensed clinicians.

📋

Full Audit Trail

Every routing decision is logged with timestamp, signal weights, and confidence scores. Full audit log accessible to health authority administrators.

🚨

Emergency Override

Any detected emergency indicator triggers an immediate redirect to 911 guidance. This override cannot be disabled by configuration or user input.

⬡

AI Transparency

All AI-assisted routing decisions include confidence scores and reasoning summaries. Users are always informed they are interacting with an AI navigation system.

🔐

Privacy by Design

No personal health information is retained after session close. Session data is encrypted in transit and processed in compliance with Nova Scotia PHIA.
