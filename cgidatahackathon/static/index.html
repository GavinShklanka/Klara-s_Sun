      How this suggestion was made

      Based on the information you provided—symptoms, duration, location, and current service availability—one option is suggested as a possible next step. This is navigation guidance only. A clinician can help confirm the most appropriate care for your situation.

      Why this helps emergency care

      Emergency departments stay available for life-threatening situations when other services are used for concerns that may not require the ED. In Nova Scotia, a portion of emergency department visits may be suitable for other settings. Using the suggested option when appropriate helps keep emergency care ready for those who need it most.

      How you're helping Nova Scotia

      Choosing a suggested pathway when appropriate helps the system use existing services effectively. This tool does not create new demand; it helps direct people to possible next steps. That can mean better use of resources and improved access to care across the province.
